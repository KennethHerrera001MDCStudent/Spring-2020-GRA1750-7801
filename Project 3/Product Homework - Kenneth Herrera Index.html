Lenovo Yoga 2 11

The Lenovo Yoga 2 11 is an Ultrabook-class convertible device, named Yoga for its ability to assume multiple form factors due to a hinged screen; as such it can be used as both a tablet and laptop computer. The Yoga 2 11 has a subtle rubber trim around the edge of its top half in order to prevent slipping on hard surfaces when in tent mode. The following are the four forms the Lenovo Yoga 2 11 can take.

360-Degree Flip-and-Fold Design

Laptop Mode

Tablet Mode

Tent Mode

Stand Mode

Lenovo Yoga 2 11 Battery

The Lenovo Yoga 2 11 can hold up to 8 hours (34 Wh) of battery life on a single charge, meaning you can go all day without plugging in, making the Yoga 2 perfect for doers on the go. Battery life can retain 6 hours of web browsing or HD playback @150 Nits. The adapter is lightweight and its charging port is small and easy to replace.

Lenovo Yoga 2 11 Construction

The Lenovo Yoga 2 11's CPU is the Intel Pentium N3540 (2.16 GHz 2 MB) with a 4 GB DDR3L 1333 MHz memory drive, and a 500 GB hard disc drive. The Lenovo Yoga 2 11's operating system is Windows 8.1 64, has Bluetooth 4.0 (802.11b/g/n), and a Intel HD graphics card.

The Lenovo Yoga 2 11 also has a 11.6 in (29 cm), 1366×768 LED-backlit touchscreen display, a pair of Dolby® Home Theater® v4 stereo speakers with microphone, a dual-layer AccuType keyboard with mousepad, and a 720p HD fixed focus web camera.

The Lenovo Yoga 2 11's port connectors include one USB 3.0, one USB 2.0, an Audio Combo Jack (headphone and mic), one micro HDMI-out, and a 2-in-1 SD/MMC card reader. The Lenovo Yoga 2 11 is made of aluminum, glass fiber, and magnesium alloy, dimensions: 298 x 207 x 17.2 mm (11.73" x 8.15" x 0.68"), and weighs 1.43 kg (3.2 lbs).
